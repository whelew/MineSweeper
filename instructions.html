How to Play!

  • Minesweeper is a game where mines are hidden in a grid of tiles. The aim of the game is to reveal all safe tiles!
  • To reveal a tile left click it!
  • Safe tiles will have a number on them from 1 - 8, this number tells you how many mines are around that tile!
  • A mine can be touching a tile horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
  • Sometimes a tile will have no number inside, this means that it is touching no mines. This will also reveal safe tiles around it until it hits a numbered tile.
  • Right click on your mouse to place a flag on a tile you think contains a mine!
  • The amount of flags you start with tells you how many mines there are in the game!
  • If you hit a mine, you lose the game!!!
  • To win the game you simply need to reveal every safe tile!!!
  • You score is based off of the timer and how long you take to complete the level!
  • Good Luck and have Fun!
